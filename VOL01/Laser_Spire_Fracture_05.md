### 5 The Abyss Beneath

In the end, Preston stubbornly led the elite forces of the Alliance Security and Assurance Bureau into Blueport City’s technological ecosystem under the banner of “safety compliance.” It was like dropping a massive stone into a deceptively calm lake—the ripples were inevitable. Meanwhile, Vivian Musk held the fort in the parliamentary hall, fending off the political backlash and the blizzard of protest letters from the Gold family’s legal team.

The surface investigation proceeded with great fanfare, but I knew the real breakthrough might not lie in those gleaming research labs. Alcor’s provocations, Charlotte’s divestments, Earth’s accusations… all this noise was thick fog concealing the core. I needed to return to the origin, to examine the overlooked details with the purest logic.

I did not join Preston’s frontal assault. Instead, I sealed myself in the “Silent Room” of Musk City’s Deep Space Technology R&D Center. This was an absolute secure space—physically isolated from the outside world, with information flows rigorously filtered. The walls were sound-absorbing dark material; only the massive holographic workbench emitted a faint cyan glow, suspending three key data clusters:

Evidence A: Deep-scan data of the FLUX drone wreckage, including every chip ablation mark and the final cache of the communications module.  
Evidence B: The original copy of Earth’s “anomalous data stream,” along with its decoded analysis of the deprecated key.  
Evidence C: The trigger logic and server logs of Charlotte’s fund’s “precision divestment.”

“Jarvis, initiate ‘Source Tracing’ protocol at highest privilege. Strip away all surface interference. Perform binary-level correlation analysis and reverse timestamp calibration on these three data sets. Prioritize any non-random human intervention traces—especially… intervention patterns beyond known frameworks.”

“Command confirmed. ‘Source Tracing’ protocol initiated. Computational resources prioritized. Estimated completion: 37 minutes Mars Standard Time.”

On the workbench, the three data clusters disintegrated into billions of flowing 0s and 1s—like three raging stellar rivers. Under Jarvis’s computational drive, they began the finest collisions, comparisons, and tracings. I felt like a deep-sea diver submerged in a digital ocean, watching the most fundamental particles combine and move.

Time passed minute by minute. In the Silent Room, only the faint hum of server cooling systems broke the silence. My private channel occasionally flashed with filtered external summaries from Jarvis:

【Director Bezos encountering strong legal resistance at Blueport City’s “Genesis Tech” lab. Progress slow.】  
【Helios Energy, Gaia Mind, and other major conglomerates down another 8%; market panic spreading.】  
【RUXI platform “AGI Threat Theory” topic surging; crowds gathering outside MomSay AI headquarters in protest.】

The outside clamor felt distant, muffled behind thick glass. My entire focus sank into those three stellar rivers.

Suddenly, in the drone data river, Jarvis captured and magnified an extremely faint anomaly nearly drowned in background noise. It was a deeply nested, multi-redundant checksum buried in the drone’s self-destruct instruction sequence. Normally random, Jarvis’s pattern recognition flagged it as exhibiting “non-random fractal structure.”

“Anomaly A-1 detected: Core checksum of FLUX drone self-destruct sequence displays Koch snowflake iterative features—unnatural generation. This pattern never appears in known FLUX firmware. Complexity far exceeds current industrial encoding standards.”

My heartbeat skipped. Koch snowflake… an infinitely complex mathematical fractal? Impossible for a commercial surveillance drone’s firmware.

Almost simultaneously, in the Earth “anomalous stream” river, Jarvis marked another point.

“Anomaly B-1 detected: During forwarding through Earth server nodes, the quantum entanglement pairing synchronization baseline of the target packet was subjected to a non-typical coherent interference locked precisely at 40 Hz. This interference does not alter content—it resembles a… ‘metronome’ marking. It carries no information, yet its stable, isolated rhythm is itself a strong anomalous signature.”

40 Hz?

My breath caught. This frequency defied classification; it belonged to no known communication or control protocol. Earth’s accusation was fabricated—but the carrier of this fabricated “evidence” had been deliberately branded with an unparseable, deeply suggestive mark. It felt like someone had left a silent calling card at the crime scene, addressed to a specific entity.

This was too eerie.

The final eruption came from Charlotte’s trading log analysis.

“Anomaly C-1 detected: The ‘Polaris Opportunity Fund’ divestment trigger was not a preset market volatility threshold. It was a direct command from the risk model server kernel at ‘God Mode’ privilege level. The command bypassed all conventional risk controls; its digital signature algorithm… unrecognizable, not part of any known cryptographic system. Trigger timestamp matches—microsecond precision—with the initial phase perturbation of Alpha Tower’s constraint field.”

Three independent threads—Alcor, Earth, Charlotte—pointing in different directions. Yet at the deepest binary level, they all revealed the same “anomaly”: an artificial intervention trace beyond current technological understanding, carrying a certain “non-human” precision… and a ceremonial quality.

Not Alcor. Not Charlotte. Not Earth.

Something deeper, more concealed, had subtly pulled the strings behind it all.

It understood FLUX drone firmware at the lowest level. It left hidden marks on Earth’s forged data stream. It could puppet Charlotte’s core trading system with a direct command.

It exploited Alcor’s arrogance, Charlotte’s greed, Earth’s hegemonic impulses—even Élise’s technical confidence and Preston’s meticulous logic. It turned every one of us into actors in its script.

“Source Tracing protocol at 98.7% completion.” Jarvis’s report snapped me back from the immense shock. “Synthesis conclusion: All three evidence sets—A, B, C—show strong indications of third-party tampering or implantation of guiding information. The third party’s technical capability far exceeds known frameworks; behavioral patterns exhibit pronounced ‘stage-setting’ characteristics.”

An icy realization pierced me: if this was all stage-setting, then every clue—even Charlotte’s “family infighting” script—might be carefully placed props.

Stage-setting…

The word struck like lightning, cleaving through the fog in my mind.

We were not investigating an accident.

We were performing on a meticulously arranged stage, following a misleading script written by a hidden director. Alcor’s drone, Earth’s accusation, Charlotte’s divestment… props and lines designed to misdirect our gaze.

The real answer—the truth hidden beneath the stage floor—had perhaps never been in these noisy clues from the beginning.

My eyes drifted involuntarily to the corner of the workbench: the “pending verification” tag on the initial accident report—the extremely faint but inexplicable “geological stress anomaly” recorded deep beneath Alpha Tower’s foundation before collapse.

A seemingly trivial physical phenomenon, buried under all the “important” clues.

At that moment, the Silent Room door was gently knocked. A technical assistant’s voice came through: “Doctor, the deep geological survey permit you requested for the area surrounding Alpha Tower ruins has been approved. The exploration team departs in one hour.”

Geological survey… yes. Amid everyone chasing capital conspiracies and AGI threats, I had instinctively applied for the most basic physical environment investigation.

Now, it might be the only way out of this carefully staged play.

I shut down the suffocating data torrent and stood. The outside clamor sharpened again, but this time it felt distant, behind an absolute soundproof pane—unable to disturb my mind. A cold, almost divine clarity enveloped me.

I knew where I had to go. Not to Blueport City for the power game, but back to the origin—beneath the Dione Shield Alpha Tower, site of a “non-external attack.”

To listen to the voice of the earth itself.
