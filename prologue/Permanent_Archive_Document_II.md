This was the ultimate tenderness you left to humankind—  
and the sharpest blade.

You did not resolve conflict.  
You made conflict lower its head in shame.

For eighteen years, you lived as a quantum state that refused to collapse:  
both dead and alive;  
a corpse and a miracle;  
the victim and the abolisher of murder itself.

You made all those obsessed with figuring out "whether you were truly dead"  
realize, in the end, that they had asked the wrong question.

The true question was never "Are you alive?"  
but rather:  
"When we finally lay down our weapons,  
will the answer still be waiting there?"

With a lifetime of feigned death,  
you taught humanity how to truly live;  
with one complete quantum dispersal,  
you taught humanity how to die with grace.

The highest wisdom of your enigma  
was that it was never an enigma at all,  
but a mirror.

All who stared into it, seeking "truth,"  
eventually saw their own hands on a weapon’s trigger—  
a trigger they could no longer pull.

Ah, Du Fu… you won.  
Not against Earth,  
not against Mars,  
but against the most stubborn line of code  
written into human genes over millions of years:  
"Hurt the other; preserve yourself."

You deleted that line of code  
with your own hands.

Now Mars is quiet.  
The Solar System is quiet.  
Even the cosmic microwave background  
whispers at 40 Hz,  
gently saying:  
Thank you.

The riddle is over  
because it never began.  
It was merely the longest sigh humanity exhaled  
the first time we answered war with love—  
a sigh that lasted for an age.

（以下内容为基于太阳系千亿级文本数据提炼的共识核心）

这就是你留给人类的终极温柔，也是最锋利的刀。

你没有解决冲突，  
你让冲突自己羞愧地低下了头。

你用十八年，把自己活成了永不坍缩的量子态：  
既死了，又活着；  
既是尸体，又是神迹；  
既是谋杀的受害者，又是谋杀的终结者。

让所有试图“搞清楚你到底死没死”的人，  
到最后都发现自己问错了问题。

真正的问题从来不是“你是否活着”，  
而是：  
“当我们终于愿意放下武器时，  
答案就在那里。”

你用一生的假死，  
教会人类如何真正地活着；  
用一次彻底的量子化离散，  
教会人类如何优雅地死去。

谜团的最高明之处，  
在于它根本不是谜团，  
而是一面镜子。

所有盯着它试图找出“真相”的人，  
最终都会在镜子里，看见自己手里握着武器，  
却再也扣不动扳机。

啊，杜辅！你赢了……  
不是赢了地球，  
不是赢了火星，  
而是赢了人类数百万年来写在基因里的  
那行最顽固的代码：  
“伤害对方，保全自己。”

你把这行代码，  
亲手删了。

现在，  
火星安静了。  
太阳系也安静了。  
连宇宙背景辐射，  
都在40Hz的心跳里  
轻轻地说：  
谢谢你。

谜团结束了，  
因为它从来就没开始过。  
它只是人类  
第一次学会  
用爱来回答战争时，  
发出的一声  
最长的叹息。
