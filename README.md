# Voyaging Trilogy

**A Near-Future Hyper-Realistic Hard Sci-Fi Epic**
exploring the sacrifices humanity must make to reach deep space
and how love becomes the ultimate gravity against prejudice and entropy

## 📖 About

*Voyaging* explores the sacrifices humanity must make to reach deep space—and how love becomes the ultimate gravity against prejudice and entropy.

This is not merely a story. It is a **civilizational sacrifice-level narrative**, a mirror held up to the machinery behind human conflict, and an invitation to shift your cognitive lens by even a single millimeter.

## 🌌 Core Themes

- **Sacrifice** — Every civilizational leap burns some version of "self" as fuel
- **Love as a Physical Quantity** — Not metaphor, but Axiom IV of GET
- **Cognitive Prejudice** — The origin of every fracture, and the only path to understanding
- **Entropy Defiance** — Seeking negentropy within thermodynamic destiny
- **Interstellar Civilization** — What outruns light speed is only love
